Lists

There are two types of list . First is ordered list and the second one is unordered list

First ordered list , in this the list is arranged in an order. In this we use the tag 'ol'.

  1. Coffee
  2. Tea
  3. Milk

Second one is unordered list, in the list is not arranged in an order. In this we use the tag'ul'.

  • Coffee
  • Tea
  • Milk
